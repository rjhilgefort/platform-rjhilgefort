## Quick Tips

1. **Find images:** Your phone photos, stock sites (unsplash.com), or bourbon brand press kits
2. **Resize:** Keep under 500KB per image (use tinypng.com to compress)
3. **Name correctly:** Use exact names from checklist (lowercase, hyphens)
4. **Drop in folders:** Place in correct `/public/images/[folder]/` location
5. **Refresh browser:** Images appear automatically, no code changes needed
